AxomiTV


About the App


The AxomiTV is an Android App with the main purpose to let users to watch Assamese TV Channels Live right on their Smart Phone. AxomiTV is packed with some awesome features like fullscreen view of channels, switch channels from one place etc. The main purpose of AxomiTV is to never let miss any Assamese show when you are not at home. You just open AxomiTV on your Smart phone and start watching live.

Features

  • No Registration. Just Open and start streaming.
  • Simple UI. Easy to use.

  • Highly Optimised, Small Size.
  • Ad hide feature.
  • Works on all Network.
  • Supports on Android 5.0 (Lolipop) and all newer android version.
